A Reflection on Purpose & Values

UNC Foundation

Vision

To be the top university of choice for Bicolanos everywhere, nurturing students through empowering, outcome-based education, to help them become purposeful, productive, and future-ready human beings who will contribute to the sustainable development of Bicol and better tomorrows for all.
UNC Foundation

Mission

To expand opportunities for every Bicolano everywhere and prepare them for a purposeful life, by providing empowering outcome-based education and a nurturing learning environment.
UNC Foundation

Core Values

  • We Champion Excellence
    We consistently strive for excellence in everything we do.
  • We Nurture Dreams
    We passionately guide and inspire our students to leverage their potentials and aspire for better lives.
  • We Do The Right Things Right
    We uphold integrity in everything we do. We hold ourselves to high standards of personal accountability and character. We do things right.
  • We Are Dynamic and Creative
    We anticipate the forces of change. We explore possibilities with intent and purpose.
  • We Respect Each Other and Work As A Team
    We collaboratively maximize our talents and capabilities. We hold each other in high regard and passionately realize our shared purpose, priorities and promises.
SCIS Excellence

Graduate Attributes

  • Excellent Communicator
    Ability to effectively use oral and written skills in sharing and receiving updated and verified information through various communication channels.
  • Creative Leader
    Ability to critically think of solutions and strategies that contribute to making great decisions and innovations.
  • Ethically Responsible Citizen
    Ability to harmoniously live and work with others with trust, dignity and integrity promoting respect for human rights, multicultural understanding, and preservation of cultural heritage.
  • Lifelong Learner
    Ability to up-skill, cross-skill and re-skill himself/herself, learn independently, reflect, and adapt with the societal changes/demands.

My Personal Reflection

As an SCIS student at the University of Nueva Caceres, these values are not merely words on paper—they are the compass that guides my academic journey and shapes who I am becoming. Every class, every project, every challenge is an opportunity to embody these principles.

The mission to provide empowering, outcome-based education resonates deeply with me. I see it in action when professors encourage us to think critically, when we're pushed to solve real-world problems through code, and when we're given the freedom to innovate. This isn't just about learning programming languages—it's about becoming a purposeful, productive, and future-ready individual.

The core value of "We Nurture Dreams" particularly speaks to my heart. As someone passionate about technology and its potential to transform lives, I feel supported in exploring my aspirations. SCIS doesn't just teach us technical skills; it cultivates our ability to dream bigger and reach higher. The emphasis on being a Lifelong Learner reminds me that graduation is not the end, but the beginning of a continuous journey of growth.

Being part of SCIS means embracing the responsibility of becoming an Ethically Responsible Citizen in the digital age. In a field where our work can impact millions, integrity isn't optional—it's essential. The values of excellence, teamwork, and doing things right have become my personal standard, pushing me to contribute meaningfully to both my community in Bicol and the broader tech ecosystem.

These aren't just institutional values—they are becoming my values, shaping the professional and person I aspire to be.

— An SCIS Student's Journey
